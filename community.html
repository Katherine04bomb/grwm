June 2025 VISA UPDATE
🇦🇺 Australia still requires a visa — but 144hr transit is available
Australian passport holders need to apply for a tourist visa before entering China. Processing takes 4–7 days. However you CAN do a 144-hour visa-free transit through Shanghai, Beijing or Guangzhou if you have an onward ticket. Check the Get Ready page for the full visa guide.
May 2025 K'S TIP
💳 Alipay now works much better for foreigners
Big update — Alipay has made it significantly easier to link foreign Visa and Mastercard. No more needing a Chinese bank account as an intermediate step. Download the international version of the app and go to Settings → Bank Cards. Takes about 5 minutes.
April 2025 TRAVEL NEWS
🌸 Best time to visit Beijing: April–May right now!
Spring in Beijing is stunning — cherry blossoms at the Summer Palace, perfect temperatures (15–22°C), and fewer tourists than summer. If you're planning a Beijing trip, April and May are the sweet spot. Book accommodation early as it fills up fast.
